Description of Our Services

Chat Bot

A chatbot or chatterbot is a software application used to conduct an on-line chat conversation via text or text-to-speech, in lieu of providing direct contact with a live human agent. A chatbot is a type of software that can help customers by automating conversations and interact with them through messaging platforms.

Voice Search

Voice search, also called voice-enabled, allows the user to use a voice command to search the Internet, a website, or an app. In a broader definition, voice search includes open-domain keyword query on any information on the Internet, for example in Google Voice Search, Cortana, Siri and Amazon Echo.Voice search is often interactive, involving several rounds of interaction that allows a system to ask for clarification. Voice search is a type of dialog system.

Analyze user behavior

Behavioral analytics utilizes a combination of big data analytics and artificial intelligence on user behavioral data to identify patterns, trends, anomalies, and other useful insights to enable appropriate actions. Behavioral analytics is used in many industries and applications including ecommerce, healthcare, banking, insurance, and cybersecurity.

Disadvantages

The down side to implementing artificial intelligence is that since AI has proven to be more efficient, it will replace the majority of job positions. In other words it will replace real humans. Also, since analysis with AI has unlimited potential to extract data and ideas from customers, that can cause some privacy issues.
Nonetheless, these minor issues don't outweigh the advantages that companies can obtain by implementing artificial intelligence into their websites

Advantages

User experience. If a visitor to your website can not use it efficiently and effectively, he will leave your website. Many client-oriented web applications are starting to rely on AI in order to improve customer experience
Personalized Content. By implementing AI on your website, you can show to users certain content that is designed specifically for them. AI also can recommend them new content being it either music, video or some other content based on their past behavior and interested.
Voice Search Optimization. The fastest trending feature is voice search. AI can help to optimize website voice search for different mobile devices, since, the major customer base is accessing websites through their mobile phones.
AI as a UX. As we talked earlier, the most common example is a chatbot.

Why

AI can be expensive at first to implement, however, it will pay for itself after a certain period of time. With an AI on your website, the company will get a higher flow of customers. And, since AI will recommend them products based on their searches, our clients will spend more money and spend less time of searching for a desirable product. Additionally, with AI customers will have a better experience, thus they most likely to stay with other company rather than searching for another.
